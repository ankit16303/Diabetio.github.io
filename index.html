Diabetes Management Service


Oxyjon uses technology and specialized health coaches to improve health outcomes for the patients through changes in diet, lifestyle, and higher adherence to medication.
Our guidelines are based on recognised national and international medical institutions. Patients working with us have seen a decrease of 1.5% in their HBA1C values over a period of 6 months.

About Us

Treatment of Diabetes requires a combination of medication, scientific diet plan, regular testing and exercise routine to effectively manage the sugar levels. The typical Indian diet is high in Carbohydrates, Fats and therefore we create a diet chart with the right mix of macronutrients and calorie balance across meals.

Diabetio creates knowledge about and treatments for diseases that are among the most chronic, costly, and consequential for patients, their families, and the Nation.
